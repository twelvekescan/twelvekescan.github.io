Company Profile

Twelve Guest Home is registered in Canada as a federal incorporation. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Sichuan Twelve Guest Culture Creative Co., Ltd. in China.

Scope of Services

Twelve Guest Home mainly provide accommodation services similar to Airbnb, but with more expanded area. We approach individual landlords and property management companies to rent their properties to local residents and foreign travellers.
